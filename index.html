Aidan Markham Games Resume Twitter ☰

Aidan Markham is an independent video game developer, artist, and co-founder of Aesthetician Labs.

Alongside the rest of Aesthetician Labs, he won first place in the 2018 New York State Game Dev Challenge, securing $12,000 in funding and incubation to continue development on Crazy Platez, a high-octane food delivery mobile game.

He graduated from Rochester Institute of Technology with a B.S. in Game Design & Development in the fall of 2019.

In July of 2020, he gave a workshop how to get started with shaders in unity.

In July of 2021, he gave a workshop how to get players to finish your jam game. It also exists as a blog post!

He is currently working on more great games with Aesthetician Labs, alongside many other creative projects.
